Goal

These lessons are your semester-long class project; they are meant to be done online outside of class time. Each week you will build towards a final objective using skills and experience obtained from class. You are encouraged to collaborate, work together, ask questions and in general, pretend that this is "real life".

The final objective is essentially your "final exam"; you will finish this class with a completed web-application, using a database layout that you have designed, data that you have populated, and queries that you have written. Your peers and I will grade your work and your class participation.
