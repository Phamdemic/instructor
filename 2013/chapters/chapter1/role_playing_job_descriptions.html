CSC 191: Chapter 1 - Job Descriptions

The class will be divided into three groups for the sake of role-playing during in-class labs. At first, each student will be placed into a random group; minor changes might be required to further balance the groups. The following job descriptions are taken from page 241 in the book.

Account Managers (AM)
Account Managers are responsible for the company's marketing activities; for example, promotions based on customers' previous purchases or for specific books.

  • decide which books to purchase
  • determine price and promotions

Customer Service Representatives (SR)
Customer Service Representatives add new customers and enter orders in the database.

  • can correct/adjust orders and order items
  • can add/update customer details

Data Entry Clerks (DE)
Data Entry Clerks enter inventory updates like adding new books and publishers, change prices, and so on.

  • responsible for general data input (books, authors, publishers)
  • responsible for database integrity (constraints, primary/foreign keys)
